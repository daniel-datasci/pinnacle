Organizational Effectiveness Assessment Survey

This survey is about the organizational effectiveness exercise the Institute of Industrial and Organizational Psychology is conducting for Pinnacle Oil and Gas. This survey is part of the instruments to assess the current culture and climate in the organization, so as to improve staff quality, retention of skills, talent management and consequently reposition Pinnacle as an employer of choice.

This survey will take you between 15-20 mins and all of the information that you provide will be treated as highly confidential and will only be used for research purposes. Your comments will not be identified as belonging to you; instead, they will be combined with those gathered from other survey participants, and will be analyzed as part of a group.

The Survey is divided into three sections; please ensure to respond to all the questions.

Section 2

Please Read the Instruction before You Start.
This section consists of six questions. Each question has four alternatives. Divide 100 points among these four alternatives depending on the extent to which each alternative is similar to what obtains in Pinnacle. Give a higher number of points to the alternative that is most similar to Pinnacle. For example, in question one, if you think alternative A is very similar to Pinnacle, alternative B and C are somewhat similar, and alternative D is hardly similar at all, you might give 55 points to A, 20 points to B and C, and 5 points to D. Just be sure your total equals 100 points for each question.

Note: The questions in this section will be shared into six groups, with each group containing four questions. For each group, the first pass through of questions is labeled “Now”. This refers to the culture, as it exists today. After you complete the “Now” for a group, you will find the questions repeated under a heading of “Preferred”. Your answers to these questions should be based on how you would like the organization to look five years from now.
